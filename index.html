Our Story

Founded in 1952 by master jeweler Ryaan Al-Farsi, our family has dedicated three generations to the art of fine jewelry craftsmanship. What began as a small workshop in Dubai's gold souk has grown into an internationally acclaimed maison, yet we remain committed to the personal touch that defines true luxury.

Every piece is more than jewelry—it's a story waiting to be told, a memory to be cherished, a legacy to be passed down.
